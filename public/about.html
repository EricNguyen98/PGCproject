About

This is a project made for INST377.

How to Use

To use this tool please type in the agency of interest into the search bar and hit the 'List' button to generate a list of the corresponding offices or the 'Map' button to generate points matching the corresponding offices on the map. If you would like all PG County Offices please hit the 'Get All Offices' button.

Information Problem

Many students and faculty coming to the University of Maryland are unfamilar with PG County. On top of many of the stresses of being in an academic environment, finding the nearest PG County office to solve their problems can be a daunting task. To solve this problem we created the Offices Look Up tool. It displays a list of offices depending on the agency the user is interested in and can display each office on a map centered on UMD.
